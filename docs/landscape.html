End of the Universe (2017)

This piece is from my Digital Imaging class. The theme of this project was "the End of..." I chose the End of the Universe as my theme. In this piece, I depict what I would imagine to be a far off distant planet, light years away. Colors were based off of Super Mario Odyssey.
