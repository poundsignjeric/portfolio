Shoot for the Moon (2015)

This is a piece from my 2-D Design class at Berkeley City College. This piece was featured in a Student Art Show in 2015. The theme was a self portrait with Graphic Design Elements. I chose to incorporate an inspirational message. This piece was done digitally in Adobe Illustrator.
